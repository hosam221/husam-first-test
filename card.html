Blue Discus Fish

I guess it’s really the experience of the whole thing that remains. Like Melville wrote about in that great whale/fish story Moby Dick. Man vs. Nature.

  • Scientific Name: Blue Discus
  • Avarage Length: 10 centimeters
  • Avarage Lifespan: 3 Years
  • Habitat: Torpical coral reefs

To me the blue discus represents the deep blue sea, and all the stories in my mind about past fishing forays, especially the trip I took off North Carolina’s Outer Banks when we went searching for the big one…the Blue Marlin.
